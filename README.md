
# Rapping Creepers

A minecraft mod made in honour of Dan Bull, and his contribution to Minecraft Community.

# Features
* **Music Discs**  with og and instrumental versions of Creeper Rap
* **Upcomming - Creeper DJ** Musical Creeper, playing his favourite tune, encouraging his mates in most frightful way
